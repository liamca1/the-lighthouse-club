We invite you to the inauguration of Fandango in the lighthouse from sunrise to midnight on Sunday, 30th October at Plötzensee, Berlin. We kindly ask you to read our community guidelines and confirm by adding your name to the guestlist below.

Community Guidelines

Fandango is a community where we hope everyone feels a sense of responsibility for each other’s comfort. We want a space where every person feels safe to express their individuality.

Collectively creating a safe environment for free-expression requires respect and awareness from all of us. Respect for each other’s differences, personal space and pronoun. Awareness for ourselves, our consumption and actions.

We also ask you to take care of yourself during Fandango;
drink water, take breaks, stretch your legs, have a chat and most importantly, be aware of your consumption and how it affects you and others.

We have a zero-tolerance policy for any homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, sexism, or racism. Any incidents of harassment or abuse of any kind will result in immediate exit.

Always contact our dedicated safe space team if you need any help, support, or guidance.
