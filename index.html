Community Guidelines

Fandango is a community where we hope everyone feels a sense of responsibility for each other’s comfort. We want a space where every person feels safe to express their individuality.

Collectively creating a safe environment for free-expression requires respect and awareness from all of us. Respect for each other’s differences, personal space and pronoun. Awareness for ourselves, our consumption and actions.

We also ask that you take responsibility for your own actions during Fandango;
take breaks, drink water, stretch your legs, have a chat and most importantly, be aware of your consumption and how it affects you.

We have a zero-tolerance policy for any homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, sexism, or racism. Any incidents of harassment or abuse of any kind will result in immediate exit. Always contact our dedicated safe space team if you need any help, support, or guidance.
